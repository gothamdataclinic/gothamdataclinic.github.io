Steve joined Gotham Data Clinic while serving as a Program Lead for Responsible Computing at Mozilla and an Adjunct at the College of Staten Island.

Prior to his current role, Steve served as a Program Coordinator for [NYU BrainWaves Project](https://wp.nyu.edu/brainwaves).

He taught science, technology, engineering, and mathematics at the elementary and high school levels in California and New York.
